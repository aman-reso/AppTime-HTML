Welcome to AppTime (also referred to as "ScreenTime", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our application.

⚠️ PROMINENT DISCLOSURE - DATA COLLECTION AND SHARING

AppTime collects, transmits, syncs, and stores your Installed Application information (including application names, package identifiers, and usage statistics) to enable screen time tracking and app usage analytics features, when you use the app to monitor your device usage patterns.

Specifically, AppTime:

  • Collects information about all applications installed on your device, including application names and package identifiers
  • Transmits this Installed Application information to our servers to provide cloud sync, backup, and cross-device analytics features
  • Stores your Installed Application information both locally on your device and on our secure servers
  • Uses this data to generate usage reports, provide personalized insights, and improve app functionality

This data collection is essential for the core functionality of AppTime. By using this app, you consent to the collection, transmission, storage, and use of your Installed Application information as described in this Privacy Policy.

You have the right to opt-out of data transmission at any time through the app settings, though this may limit certain features such as cloud backup and cross-device synchronization.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Installed Application Information

AppTime collects and transmits your Installed Application information, which includes:

  • Application Names: The names of all applications installed on your device
  • Package Identifiers: Unique identifiers for each installed application (e.g., com.example.app)
  • Application Metadata: Information about application versions, installation dates, and categories

This information is collected automatically when you first launch the app and is continuously updated as you install or uninstall applications. This data is transmitted to our servers to enable cloud-based features.

1.2 App Usage Data

To provide you with screen time tracking and app usage analytics, we collect:

  • Time spent on each application
  • Frequency of app usage
  • Session duration and patterns
  • Device information (model, operating system version)

This usage data is linked to your Installed Application information and may be transmitted to our servers for analytics and cloud sync purposes.

1.2 Device Information

We may collect certain device information to ensure compatibility and improve our services:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Device identifiers (anonymized)

1.3 Usage Analytics

We collect aggregated, anonymized usage statistics to improve our application:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide screen time tracking and app usage statistics
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats

3. Data Storage, Transmission, and Security

3.1 Data Transmission

AppTime transmits your Installed Application information and app usage data to our secure servers. This transmission occurs:

  • When you first launch the app (to sync your installed applications)
  • Periodically to keep your data synchronized across devices
  • When you enable cloud backup features
  • To provide analytics and usage insights

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols to ensure secure transfer.

3.2 Data Storage

Your data is stored in two locations:

  • Local Storage: On your device for immediate access and offline functionality
  • Cloud Storage: On our secure servers to enable backup, sync, and cross-device access

You can control cloud storage through app settings. Disabling cloud sync will prevent data transmission to our servers, but local functionality will continue to work.

3.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • Encryption at rest for data stored on our servers
  • Secure local storage mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments and audits
  • Access controls and authentication protocols

3.4 Data Retention

Your Installed Application information and app usage data are retained:

  • Locally: According to your device storage and app settings
  • On Servers: Until you delete your account or request data deletion, or as required by law

You can delete your data at any time through the application settings or by contacting us directly.

AppTime shares your Installed Application information and usage data in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We share your Installed Application information and usage data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our application, including:

All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your data for the specific purposes of providing services to us.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your Installed Application information when required by law or to comply with legal processes, including:

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your Installed Application information and usage data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will provide notice to users before such transfer occurs.

4.4 What We Do NOT Do

We do NOT:

  • Sell, trade, or rent your Installed Application information to third parties for marketing purposes
  • Share your data with advertisers without your explicit consent
  • Use your Installed Application information for purposes other than those disclosed in this policy

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your Installed Application information and usage data:

  • Access: You can view all data collected by the application, including your Installed Application information, through the app settings
  • Opt-out of Data Transmission: You can disable cloud sync and data transmission to our servers in the app settings. This will prevent your Installed Application information from being transmitted, though local tracking will continue
  • Deletion: You can delete your usage data and Installed Application information at any time through the application settings or by contacting us
  • Account Deletion: You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data, including Installed Application information stored on our servers
  • Export: You can export your data for personal use in standard formats
  • Revoke Consent: You can revoke your consent to data collection at any time by uninstalling the app or disabling data collection features

Important: If you opt-out of data transmission, certain features such as cloud backup, cross-device sync, and remote analytics will not be available. Local tracking and basic functionality will continue to work.

6. Permissions and Data Access

Our application requires certain permissions to function properly and collect Installed Application information:

  • Usage Access (QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES / PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS): Required to access and collect information about all installed applications on your device, including application names and package identifiers. This permission is essential for the core functionality of AppTime.
  • Internet Access: Required to transmit your Installed Application information and usage data to our servers for cloud sync and analytics features.
  • Storage: To save your usage data and Installed Application information locally on your device.
  • Notification Access: To provide usage alerts and reminders (optional)

By granting these permissions, you consent to the collection and transmission of your Installed Application information as described in this Privacy Policy.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, though this will limit or disable application functionality. Revoking Usage Access will prevent the app from collecting Installed Application information, and revoking Internet Access will prevent data transmission to our servers.
