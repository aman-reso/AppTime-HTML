Privacy Policy

Welcome to AppTime (also referred to as "ScreenTime", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 App Usage Data

To provide you with screen time tracking and app usage analytics, we collect:

  • Application names and package identifiers
  • Time spent on each application
  • Frequency of app usage
  • Device information (model, operating system version)

1.2 Device Information

We may collect certain device information to ensure compatibility and improve our services:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version
  • Device identifiers (anonymized)

1.3 Usage Analytics

We collect aggregated, anonymized usage statistics to improve our application:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide screen time tracking and app usage statistics
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and resolve technical issues
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All app usage data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal app usage data to external servers without your explicit consent.

3.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure local storage mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments

3.3 Data Retention

Your app usage data is retained on your device according to your preferences. You can delete this data at any time through the application settings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our application (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all data collected by the application
  • Deletion: You can delete your usage data at any time
  • Opt-out: You can disable data collection features in settings
  • Export: You can export your data for personal use

6. Permissions

Our application requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • Usage Access: To track app usage and screen time
  • Notification Access: To provide usage alerts and reminders (optional)
  • Storage: To save your usage data locally

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, though this may limit application functionality.
